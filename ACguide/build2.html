HEAVY BUILD

Pros:
- High armor points: Heavy builds can take a lot of hits without dying.
- Strong stance: Heavy builds don't stagger easily, meaning they can attack for long periods of time without being interrupted.
- High weight limit: Much more powerful units become available with heavy builds.

Cons:
- Poor evasive maneuverability: You will likely trade more hits
- Low boost speed: Poor chase capabilities
- Higher energy consumption: Heavier parts naturally consume more energy.

LEGS

Reverse joint legs recommended for more vertical boost capabilities. Steeper drop in armor points.

Acquired mid game, mid weight options available earlier.

FORTALEZA tank leg equipment. High boost speed with high weight limit in exchange for air mobility. Tank type legs also do not require planted stance to fire heavy weaponry (more ground mobility)

Acquired mid game.
