BEGINNER'S GUIDE TO ARMORED CORE VI BUILDS

Armored Core 6: Fires of Rubicon is a fast-paced, mecha-based action game developed by FromSoftware. Set in a dystopian future, players control customizable armored mechs called Armored Cores (ACs), taking on missions for various factions in a war-torn world. The game emphasizes mech customization, allowing players to modify their machines with different parts, weapons, and equipment to suit their playstyle.

Core Mechanics

In game combat revolves around customizing your AC's stats around a given challenge, allowing the player to adjust their AC's specific weight limit, armor points (health), energy output/recovery, and boost speed (movement). Some stats scale against or with others, and this guide serves to simplify these mechanics and their relationships.
