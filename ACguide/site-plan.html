Overview

Purpose

This purpose of this site is to simplify and condense in game mechanics for the video game Armored Core 6 and display it so beginner players can view and learn from it.

Audience

My audience are people who may have just started playing Armored Core 6 and find the mechanics overwhelming. They will use my site to prepare and inform themselves so they can have a more enjoyable experience.

Content

Home page

Armored Core 6: Fires of Rubicon is a fast-paced, mecha-based action game developed by FromSoftware. Set in a dystopian future, players control customizable armored mechs called Armored Cores (ACs), taking on missions for various factions in a war-torn world. The game emphasizes mech customization, allowing players to modify their machines with different parts, weapons, and equipment to suit their playstyle.
