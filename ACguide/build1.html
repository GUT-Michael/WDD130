LIGHT BUILD

Pros:
- High boost speed: Instead of walking, your AC has the option to glide on boosters. Lighter builds generally have boost speeds between 300 - 400 kilometers per hour.
- Low energy consumption: Generator will be exhausted at a slower rate.

Cons:
- Low armor points: Light builds will die quicker. Recommend maximizing repair kit use.
- Low strain limit: To counter high evasive maneuverability, it won't take many hits for a lighter build to be staggered.
- Low weight limit: ACs with lighter builds generally have to choose between equipping lighter arms units or equipping fewer units entirely. Stat can be raised with heavier arms or legs.

LEGS

Reverse joint legs recommended for more vertical boost capabilities. Steeper drop in armor points.

Acquired halfway through chapter 1.

Firmeza leg equipment greatly recommended for general application. Significantly higher weight limit.

Acquired at end of chapter 1.
